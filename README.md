# Aegis Volatility Arbitrage: MENA Market Microstructure
**Target Proxy:** VanEck Egypt Index ETF (EGPT) | **Base Currency:** USD

## Overview
This repository serves as a quantitative research environment modeling the microstructural inefficiencies of Middle Eastern emerging markets. Unlike hyper-efficient US markets, emerging markets exhibit extreme emotional volatility, lower liquidity, and delayed information synthesis.

This engine tests the hypothesis that standard deviation expansion (volatility breakouts) in these specific markets creates highly predictable mean-reverting "snap-back" events. By calculating a rolling Z-Score against a 20-day Simple Moving Average, the algorithm mathematically isolates irrational market panic and executes statistically favorable arbitrage positions.

*Architected for Harvard University quantitative research submission.*
